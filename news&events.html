School Grants

Essex Gardens Trust offers 2 grants a year to build or significantly redesign a school garden.

Applications are limited to state funded schools & academies and are awarded on a first come, first serve basis.

The application should include your plans, financing and who will be responsible for the project.

The school should promote that EGT is funding this project & invite the charity on-site for progress updates & to view the final result.
