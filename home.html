Welcome to
Essex Gardens Trust

We're a charity of volunteers passionate about protecting gardens and green spaces in Essex in addition to conserving and rediscovering the counties gardening history.

We run a range of events for our members & the public, produce publications and inventories for local landscaping and engage in the planning and conservation plans in the local area.

We are always welcoming new members and by joining us you will meet like minded people at events and social gatherings throughout the year.
